Compics Team Website

Website For Programming And Design Team.
This Site Is For a Team For Programming ,Design and Photography.
Site Is Used To Show information About their Business And Display Their Projects.
Projects Are Organized Into 4 Categories.
Users Can Register An Account And Login To Order Servies From The Team And Mention The Expected Budget ,Number Of Pages They Need And Their Preferable Colors They Need In The Design.
They Can Show All Their Preivous Orders.
Control Panel For Managing The Website. Allowing Admin To Add / Edit And Delete Technologies They Use. Allowing him To Select When Adding / Editing a Project He Add.
Admin Can Add New Projects Display With a slide Show For Each Projects Display . And ability To Add Live Preview / Download Link For This Projects.
The Projects Preview Also Contains a List of Preselected Technologies.
The Site Also Show Information About the Team Who Can Be Dynamically Added And Managed By The Admin.
Admin Also Can Show All Comming Orders.
