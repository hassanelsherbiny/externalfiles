Real Estate

Website To Advert and Reserve Real Estates
Users Can Register an Account And Login To Advert There Own Real Estates Adding Information About the Estates Like Location, Address, Area And The Price.
They Can Also add Previewing Images FOr the Estate. Estates Can Be Added For Sale Or Rent With Different Estates Types.
Only Estates Accepted By Admin Can Appear On The Site For The Surfing Users Allowing Them to Reserve The Previewed Estate Without Knowing Any Thing About The Person Who Had Added The Advertise This Give Opportunity For The admin To Manage The Appointments Between The Adviser And The Customer.
Users Can Send Messages To Admin For Communication Or Requesting Different Services .
Users Has The Ability To Categorize the Estates With Buy Or Rent Options And They Can Also Search In Specific Location, Address Or Estate Types.
